Classic tomato spaghetti

Description

describe the recipe

Ingredients

These are the ingredients needed

  • Garlic
  • Red chilli
  • Basil
  • Dried spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • plum tomatoes
  • Parmesan cheese (o)

Process

Step by step direction for suckers.

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Boil the spaghetti
  3. Add oil to a pan
  4. Add the vegetables to the oil and add seasoning
  5. Serve pasta with sauce and grated parmesan on top
