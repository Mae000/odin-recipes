Japanese Rice Curry

Description

Describe the recipe

Ingredients

Ingredients required includes:

  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Yukon gold potatoes
  • Kabocha squash
  • Ginger
  • Bell pepper
  • Asparagus spears
  • japanese eggplant
  • King oyster mushrooms
  • Button mushrooms
  • Tomato
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Vegetable stock
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Bay leaf
  • Unsalted butter
  • japanese Curry Ronx
  • apple
  • Cooked japanese short grain rice

Process

Follow the step by step direction

  1. Cut all the vegetables
  2. Oil in frying pan
  3. Fry the vegetables
  4. Add vegetable oil
  5. Add curry and cook more
  6. Eat with white rice
